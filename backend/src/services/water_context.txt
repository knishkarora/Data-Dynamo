### 💡 Killer Insights for Pitch
1. 🔴 **The Punjab Crisis**: At **156% extraction**, Punjab is in a state of severe over-exploitation. The state is literally pumping out 50% more water than it recharges annually.
2. 🟡 **The Borderline Zone**: Uttar Pradesh and Tamil Nadu are hovering around the **70% mark**. These are future risk zones where semi-critical status could quickly escalate without intervention.
3. 🟢 **The "Safe" Paradox**: While most states appear green, many are missing robust recharge data, leading to an incomplete picture of true water security.
4. 📈 **Future Risk**: States like Haryana and Rajasthan are also well over 100%, indicating a systemic water management failure across the breadbasket of India.
